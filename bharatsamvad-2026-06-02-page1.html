    <h2 class="headline">India-Oman CEPA Takes Effect: Duty-Free Trade Era Begins</h2>

    <p class="subdeck">Landmark agreement grants Indian exporters near-total duty-free access to Oman while offering cheaper imports to Indian consumers. Expected to add $2 billion to bilateral trade in next 2-3 years.</p>

    <div class="byline"><b>Special Correspondent</b> · New Delhi</div>

    <div class="body">

      <p class="lead">The India-Oman Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) officially came into force on Monday, ushering in a new chapter of enhanced economic cooperation between the two nations.</p>

      <p>The pact provides duty-free market access for thousands of products, dramatically boosting opportunities for Indian exporters while delivering cost benefits to Indian consumers through select Omani imports.</p>

     

      <h3>What Gets Cheaper in India</h3>

      <p>Omani dates will now enter India under quota-based duty concessions, offering immediate relief to consumers. Tariff reductions have also been extended to certain marble products and petrochemical items. Sensitive sectors such as gold, silver, jewellery, dairy, cereals, edible oils, spices, and footwear remain protected.</p>

 

      <h3>Major Boost for Indian Exporters</h3>

      <p>Under the CEPA, 99.38% of India’s exports by value will now enjoy duty-free access to Oman, a sharp rise from just 15.33% earlier. Key sectors set to benefit include textiles & garments, gems & jewellery, engineering goods, pharmaceuticals, leather products, footwear, processed foods, marine products, electronics, and agriculture.</p>

 

      <h3>Pharma and Electronics Get Strong Push</h3>

      <p>India’s pharmaceutical sector stands to gain significantly with zero-duty access for medicines, vaccines, pharmaceutical ingredients, and major antibiotics. Electronics exports also have huge potential, as Oman imported $1.7 billion worth of electronics in 2025 while India’s share remains modest at $146 million.</p>

 

      <h3>Big Wins for Indian Professionals</h3>

      <p>The agreement significantly improves mobility for Indian professionals. Business visitors can now stay up to 90 days, independent professionals up to 180 days, and intra-corporate transferees up to four years. The quota for corporate transfers has been increased from 20% to 50%.</p>

 

      <h3>Strategic Importance</h3>

      <p>Commerce Minister Piyush Goyal stated that the deal will create jobs, strengthen manufacturing, and enhance the competitiveness of Indian industries. Bilateral trade currently stands at $11.18 billion. Officials expect an additional $2 billion in trade over the next two to three years.</p>

     

      <p>For Oman, the agreement provides access to one of the world’s fastest-growing major economies. For India, it unlocks Oman’s $28 billion import market and serves as a strategic gateway to the Gulf Cooperation Council and East Africa.</p>

function downloadAsPDF() {

    let headline = document.querySelector(".headline") ?

                   document.querySelector(".headline").textContent.trim() : "Article";

   

    let cleanTitle = headline

        .replace(/[^a-zA-Z0-9\s-]/g, "")

        .replace(/\s+/g, "-")

        .toLowerCase()

        .substring(0, 60);

   

    const filename = "bharatsamwad-2026-06-02-page1-" + cleanTitle + ".pdf";

 

    const toast = document.createElement("div");

    toast.style.cssText = "position:fixed;bottom:30px;left:50%;transform:translateX(-50%);background:#1a1a1a;color:white;padding:14px 28px;border-radius:6px;z-index:10000;font-size:1rem;";

    toast.textContent = "Opening Print to PDF...";

    document.body.appendChild(toast);

 

    setTimeout(() => {

        toast.remove();

        window.print();

    }, 600);

}

    b.classList.toggle("show", b.dataset.lang === l);

  });

  document.querySelectorAll(".langbar button").forEach(btn => {

    btn.classList.toggle("active", btn.dataset.l === l);

  });

}

document.addEventListener("DOMContentLoaded", function(){

  var s='en';

  try{s=localStorage.getItem('bs_lang')||'en';}catch(e){}

  setLang(s);

});

LEAD: Security agencies have stepped up surveillance of cross-border telecommunication activity after detecting the reach of unauthorized cellular and radio signals into several parts of Jammu and Kashmir. Officials believe these communication channels could be used by terrorist networks to maintain contact with handlers and facilitators operating from across the border.

According to officials, unusual signal activity has been observed in districts including Kathua, Rajouri and Poonch, with traces reportedly extending beyond immediate border areas into strategically important locations in Jammu. Investigators are examining whether these transmissions are part of a broader effort to establish resilient communication links for infiltration and terror-related operations.

Authorities say the expansion of telecom infrastructure across the border has increased the possibility of signal spillover into Indian territory. Elevated tower locations and favourable terrain conditions allow frequencies to travel significant distances, creating challenges for security monitoring and interception.

## Terrain shapes the communication challenge

Security experts point out that geography plays a decisive role in how far such transmissions can travel. The mountainous terrain of the Kashmir Valley naturally limits the penetration of external signals, whereas the open plains of Jammu provide fewer obstacles, enabling radio and cellular frequencies to travel deeper inland.

Investigators have observed that infiltration routes south of the Pir Panjal range continue to remain active areas of concern. Maintaining communication between infiltrating groups and their coordinators is often viewed as a critical component of operational planning, making telecom monitoring an important element of counter-terrorism efforts.

## Emerging technologies draw attention

Officials are also tracking the use of advanced communication platforms that blend mobile technology with radio-based transmission systems. Such technologies are capable of supporting encrypted communication, reducing the likelihood of detection through conventional monitoring methods.

Security analysts believe these systems could provide terrorist handlers with the ability to coordinate movements, relay instructions and remain connected with local contacts without relying entirely on standard telecom networks. Rapid technological adaptation by hostile actors has made communications intelligence a constantly evolving challenge.

The increasing availability of sophisticated commercial communication equipment has further complicated efforts to identify and disrupt illicit networks.

PULL: Authorities believe communication security has become a critical front in counter-terrorism operations as hostile actors increasingly rely on advanced technologies to evade detection.

## Modernising countermeasures

Officials acknowledge that existing jamming and monitoring systems were largely designed for earlier generations of communication technology. As communication methods evolve, security agencies are working to deploy more precise and adaptable systems capable of detecting and neutralising unauthorized devices without affecting legitimate civilian communications.

The proposed technologies are expected to improve real-time monitoring while minimising disruption to residents, businesses and emergency services operating in nearby areas.

Security agencies maintain that technological upgrades are essential to keeping pace with rapidly changing communication methods employed by terrorist groups and their support networks.

## Concerns over unlawful signal transmissions

Officials have also highlighted concerns regarding the transmission of signals across international boundaries without authorization. Such practices, they argue, complicate spectrum management, increase security risks and undermine efforts to maintain a regulated communication environment in sensitive border regions.

Authorities continue to monitor developments closely while coordinating technical and intelligence-based responses to emerging threats.

## Public cooperation remains a vital safeguard

Alongside technological measures, officials have emphasised the importance of community awareness. Residents in border districts and vulnerable areas have been encouraged to remain alert and promptly share information regarding suspicious activities, unidentified individuals, unexplained electronic equipment, drone sightings or unusual movement patterns.

Security agencies note that local information has played a crucial role in several successful operations in the past. Timely reporting by vigilant citizens has often helped authorities identify threats, prevent infiltration attempts and disrupt support networks.

## Security as a collective responsibility

Experts stress that countering modern security threats requires a combination of advanced technology, intelligence coordination and active community participation. While agencies continue to strengthen surveillance and monitoring capabilities, public awareness remains an indispensable component of the overall security framework.

Officials have reassured citizens that measures are being taken to address emerging communication-related threats. At the same time, they emphasise that maintaining peace and security in Jammu and Kashmir depends on a shared commitment between security forces and the public.

As hostile groups increasingly seek innovative ways to communicate and evade detection, authorities believe an alert population, supported by robust technological capabilities, remains one of the strongest defences against terrorism and cross-border threats.
